## Overview
The Trump Documents Skill provides insight into the ongoing legal and political battles surrounding Donald Trump's management and disclosure of classified government documents. It includes information about alleged mishandling, investigations, and official disclosures.

## When to use
Use this skill when you need a quick rundown on the latest findings and controversies related to Trump's handling of classified documents following his presidency.

## Instructions
1. **Check for Allegeations**: Review recent congressional hearings or press releases for any newly surfaced allegations about Trump mishandling classified documents.
2. **Examine Investigative Reports**: Look into the reports by special counsel Jack Smith and other investigative bodies to understand the evidence against Trump.
3. **Review Public Disclosures**: Check whether Trump has made any public disclosures of classified documents and their implications for legal proceedings.
